Privacy

Lava Pulse does not collect, store, or sell personal data.

Lava Pulse may request microphone access only when Sound Reactive Lava is enabled. Microphone audio is processed locally on your device to animate the lava. Audio is not recorded, stored, or sent to any server.

Lava Pulse may use in-app purchases through Apple to unlock Sound Reactive Lava. Purchase processing is handled by Apple.
